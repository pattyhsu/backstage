Method. Per city: hedonic OLS on tiered solds — log $/sf ~ condition tier (T6 ref) + log sqft + quarter FE — gives each tier's offset vs a full flip; each sale's $/sf is normalized by its tier's offset. Tiers with <8 sales dropped; untiered solds excluded; T5 treated as a single tier. Source: properties solds since Jan 2023, ≥$200K, 400–10,000 sqft; partial current month excluded.
